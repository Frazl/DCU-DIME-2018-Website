ELAIVATOR & The Environemnt

A service that ELAIVATOR brings to a company that might go unnoticed is the fact that it is good for the environment. Due to its machine learning, it can predict what floor to be at, at a particular time. This results in an awful lot less electricity being used by a company to power elevators on the long run due to the fact that the elevator already knows what floor to be at.

This drops the power used by an elevator by a massive amount per day, An average elevator would take 13.2kWh per day approx. Our ELAIVATOR should knock that massive number down to 12.1kWh. It does not seem as a massive drop when you first look at it but on the long run, it is a massive amount, which is massive for the environment as the building as a whole is using less power.
